History Of Temple

The foundation stone of Shri Mandir was laid in the year 1980 and the altar was consecrated on May 9, 1985.
On Veer Nirvana Samvat 2506, the Jain temple was built by the family of Dharmanuragi Shri Mamchand ji Jain. Tarun Jain, son of Mr. Sushil Jain, the youngest son of Mamchand ji, at the age of 11 years, suddenly his health deteriorated in August 1998. After undergoing several treatments, the child became so weak that he could not even stand up and the family members hoped for his survival. Abandoned.
In these moments of despair, I suddenly had a dream that the child would be cured a day before Diwali. Amazingly, the child became so healthy a day before Diwali that he himself got up and went to the temple to see God.
Slowly, amazing changes started happening in that child. It seemed as if some divine powers started rising in him. One day he went to the temple and marked a place and said that he has to dig here. Miracles will happen here. So much power was also generated in him that he himself started digging. After a lot of digging, due to the rise of inauspicious deeds, the work of digging had to be stopped, which lasted for almost a year.
Suddenly one day the child had a dream that at a distance of nine feet from the main gate of the temple, Shri ji was sitting fourteen feet below. On digging, on Monday, 10.06.2002, on Jyeshtha Amavasya Sheet Samvat 2528, a golden idol studded with gems about 11/2 in height appeared.
After the passage of six months, he had a dream again and on digging again as per his instructions, on the ninth day, Thursday of Veer Samvat 2529, dated 26.11.02, a statue 1008 jeweled golden statue of Shri Arhnath Bhagwan appeared. Due to which there was joy everywhere. The Harshit family seated both the idols in the altar with the rituals and started worshiping. Shri Mamchand Jain family is expecting some more miracles in future.
